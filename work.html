How I Work

I believe in a hands-on approach and working in cross-functional teams that include stakeholders as partners in the project.

zsod research is important in every project, as it helps us get to know the business and its users. Ideation takes many forms, but it's important to quickly test ideas with real users to ensure that we're on the right track.

I love developing, learning, teaching, and I enjoy working with colleagues who are honest, respectful, and provide straightforward feedback. I believe that the team is crucial, and it's important to know not just the skills but also the person behind them.

Design sprints and the double diamond process can be helpful, but it's essential to work with people you enjoy being around, as work can sometimes be challenging.
